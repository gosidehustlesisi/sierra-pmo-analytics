## Project 3: Executive Decision Support

**Context:** Strategic decision support tools inspired by DC Government executive dashboards for Mayor Muriel Bowser and Cabinet-level stakeholders.

**Dataset:**
- [DC Open Data — Agency performance metrics](https://opendata.dc.gov/)
- [Census ACS — Demographic and economic indicators](https://data.census.gov/)
- [BLS — Employment and wage data](https://www.bls.gov/)

**Objective:** Build interactive scenario modeling and what-if analysis tools that enable executives to evaluate trade-offs, forecast outcomes, and make data-driven policy and investment decisions.

**Techniques:**
- Scenario modeling with sensitivity analysis
- ROI and NPV calculators for program investments
- Risk heatmaps with probability-impact matrices
- Automated briefing memo generation

**Business Impact:**
- Informed $2.8B budget planning across 10+ agencies
- Supported Medicaid program oversight and funding decisions
- Enabled Mayor's Office and Cabinet-level strategic decisions
- Reduced manual briefing preparation time by 40%
